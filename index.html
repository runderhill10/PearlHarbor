Group reminders
🚬 No bags at Pearl Harbor — all bags must stay in your car or a locker. Leave everything at the hotel before you depart.
⏰ Arizona tickets are free but timed — book on recreation.gov now. Your 3:30 PM slot is a firm commitment. Late arrivals may forfeit entry.
📱 Pre-schedule Ubers the night before — use the Uber scheduled ride feature for the morning airport run. Don't rely on surge-pricing availability at 9:00 AM.
✈️ Book flights as a group — coordinate seats together on Hawaiian Airlines. Select same flight and book round-trip in one transaction for best availability.
🏈 Waikiki beach time is flexible — if the group wants more time at Pearl Harbor, trim beach time to 30–45 min and Uber over by 2:30 PM instead.
