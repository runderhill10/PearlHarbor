What to Book (Before You Go)
⚠️
Book all three items below as soon as possible. The USS Arizona Memorial 3:00 PM timed entry is currently available — grab it now before it fills. Hawaiian Airlines mid-morning flights and Duke's Waikiki lunch should also be secured in advance.
✈️
Morning Flight to Honolulu
Hawaiian Airlines — Book Immediately
Route OGG → HNL
Target Departure ~9:00–9:30 AM
Est. Arrival ~10:05 AM HNL
Book At HawaiianAirlines.com
⏰ Book this first — everyone in the group needs to be on the same flight. A 9:00–9:30 AM departure gets you to Waikiki by late morning with plenty of time before your 3:00 PM Pearl Harbor slot.
✈️
Return Flight to Maui
Hawaiian Airlines — Book With Morning Flight
Route HNL → OGG
Target Departure ~7:00 PM
Est. Arrival ~8:00 PM OGG
Book At HawaiianAirlines.com
📌 Book both legs at the same time. Be at HNL by 5:30 PM for your ~7:00 PM departure — Waikiki to airport traffic can be slow in the evening.
⚓
USS Arizona Memorial
Recreation.gov — Reserve Timed Entry
Target Time Slot 3:00 PM
Date June 8, 2026
Entry Fee Free (NPS site)
Book At Recreation.gov
🚨 This is your hardest-to-get reservation. Book immediately at Recreation.gov — slots for the 8:30 AM entry sell out weeks or months ahead. Each person in the group needs their own ticket.
🍽️
Duke's Waikiki — Lunch
OpenTable / Duke's Direct — Recommended
Target Time 11:30 AM
Date June 8, 2026
Cuisine Hawaiian / Seafood
Book At OpenTable.com or DukesWaikiki.com
🌊 Oceanfront patio at the Outrigger Waikiki. Reservation is recommended — especially for a group. Walk-ins may face a wait on busy summer days.
💡
Group Tips & Rules
🎒
No Bags at Pearl Harbor
Bags, purses, and backpacks are NOT permitted at the Pearl Harbor National Memorial. Leave everything at the hotel or in the rental car. Small pockets only.
🚗
Use Uber Throughout
All transport is via Uber. Have the app downloaded and a payment method saved. Request your 5:10 AM Uber the night before if possible.
⏰
7:30 AM Wake-Up
No brutal early alarm! You have a relaxed morning. Just be in the Uber by 8:15 AM to make your ~9:00 AM flight out of OGG.
🌡️
Dress Comfortably
Light layers for early morning travel. Comfortable walking shoes for Pearl Harbor. You'll also have Waikiki beach time — bring sunscreen.
📱
Have Tickets Ready
Download or screenshot your Arizona Memorial reservation QR code before you travel — you may not have reliable cell service at Pearl Harbor.
💵
Bring Cash & Card
USS Missouri admission is paid (~$30/adult). Duke's and Waikiki shops will need payment. Budget ~$100–150/person for the full day.
🗓️
Full Day Itinerary

AM
Hotel
Wake Up — Grand Wailea
No brutal early alarm — enjoy a proper morning. Grab breakfast at the resort. Travel light; no bags are allowed at Pearl Harbor later.

Transport
Request Uber to Kahului Airport (OGG)
Depart hotel around 8:15 AM. About 20–25 min to the airport. Request the Uber at 8:10 AM or schedule it the night before.

Hawaiian Airlines — OGG → HNL
~35 min inter-island flight. Arrive Honolulu around 10:05 AM. Check in early — inter-island security is quick but don't cut it close.
10:05
AM
Transport
Uber from HNL to Waikiki
About 25–30 min to Waikiki. Request immediately upon landing. Arrive around 10:30–10:45 AM — head straight to Duke's area.
10:45
AM
Free Time
🌊 Waikiki Beach — Morning
Enjoy the beach before the midday heat peaks. Walk the strip, rent beach gear, browse Kalākaua Ave shops, or just relax by the water.
11:30
AM
Lunch
🍹 Duke's Waikiki
Oceanfront Hawaiian lunch at the iconic Duke's. Great food, great views. Budget ~$30–50/person. Reservation recommended — book in advance.
12:30
PM
Free Time
🌴 Waikiki Afternoon
More beach time, a drink, or explore. You have about 1.5 hours before you need to head to Pearl Harbor. Don't wander too far.
2:15
PM
Transport
Uber to Pearl Harbor
About 30–35 min from Waikiki to Pearl Harbor. Leave by 2:15 PM to arrive comfortably before your 3:00 PM slot. Waikiki traffic can be slow — don't leave late.
2:45
PM
Main Event
Arrive Pearl Harbor — Check In
Check in at the Visitor Center and browse the exhibits and museum while you wait for your 3:00 PM time slot. Have your Recreation.gov tickets downloaded offline.
3:00
PM
Reserved
⚓ USS Arizona Memorial — Timed Entry
Your reserved 3:00 PM time slot. Take the NPS boat out to the memorial above the sunken battleship. About 75 min total experience. One of the most meaningful sites in American history.
4:30
PM
Optional
USS Missouri Battleship
If time allows, the USS Missouri is a short shuttle ride away (~$30/adult). Closes at 5:00 PM — only feasible for a quick walk-through at this point. Optional for the group.
5:00
PM
Transport
Uber to Honolulu Airport (HNL)
About 20 min from Pearl Harbor to HNL. Arrive by 5:30 PM for your ~7:00 PM departure. Check in, grab a bite at the terminal if needed.

Flight
Hawaiian Airlines — HNL → OGG
Return flight to Maui. Arrives approximately 8:00 PM at Kahului Airport (OGG).
8:00
PM

Uber Back to Grand Wailea
~30 min from OGG to the resort. Arrive back around 8:30 PM.
8:30
PM
Optional Dinner
🌺 Late Dinner at the Resort
Two great options on property at Grand Wailea:

Humuhumunukunukuāpuaʻa — The resort's signature Hawaiian seafood restaurant, set over a saltwater lagoon. Upscale and worth it. Request a late seating.

Monkeypod Kitchen (Wailea) — More casual, great craft cocktails and local plates. Later hours and more flexible for walk-ins.

Reservations recommended — especially for a group arriving at 8:30 PM.
